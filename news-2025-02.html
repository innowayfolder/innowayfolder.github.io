Driven by Quality and Innovation! Innoway Successfully Completes Equipment Delivery to Customer in New York State US, Demonstrating Brand Strength

February 2025

In February of the 25th year, Innoway completed the installation and delivery of equipment for a well-known customer in New York State, including one small piece cage rod napkins collector (with RFID sorting function). This was a deep cooperation innovation between our company and the American customer. The customer implanted chips in each napkin, and our equipment could identify the presence or absence of chips and stack them in different channels. Due to the excellent performance of the equipment, the customer purchased two more devices for their factory in Pennsylvania.

About Innoway

Innoway has over 20 years of expertise in R&D and manufacturing of automation technologies and products for flatwork finishing processes in the laundry industry. As a top-tier manufacturer in China with leading R&D capabilities in this field, the company consistently listens to customer needs and delivers products and services driven by innovation and reliable quality. Its products are widely exported to major global markets including the United States, Europe, and Southeast Asia.
